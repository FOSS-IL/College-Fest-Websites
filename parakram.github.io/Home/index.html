About us

IET LUCKNOW

Planted three decades ago, a seed in the high gardens of Lucknow to provide the shade of knowledge, it’s been enriching the youth ever since. It has served as pathfinder for a generation of engineers in their respective pursuits. Established in 1984, it marked the genesis of an academia, the pursuit of research, education, scholarship, and thus the odyssey had begun. The attitude to face competition and succeed regardless, extends to other fields by setting benchmarks and bringing laurels in cultural, technical and sports events across the country. Credit goes to the numerous teams on campus serving as a cradle for multidimensional development of students. Growing from strength to strength and been recognised as an inimitable college of North India, the institute today is a leading establishment in the field of education and research. Also, IET is reinstated with the best scholars and professors of the state,acquiring numerous laurels under its name since enactment.

PARAKRAM

The absence of indecision even in the face of death is the true mark of valor. Parakram meaning Valour or in layman terms 'Courage' has always stood to its name, overcoming every possible chance to outlast the race. Parakram is a three day technical fest organized by IETians which explores the innate talent of the students.It is a platform to think, create and exhibit innovative ideas. The winners are awarded with attractive cash prizes but the learning experience tops it off.
